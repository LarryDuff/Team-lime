What help is available?

This page provides advice and money saving tips as well as links to other resources including government help

Government support

All domestic energy customers in Great Britain will receive a £400 grant to help with the cost of their energy bills through the Energy Bill Support Scheme.
Households liable for Council Tax in Bands A-D in England will also receive a £150 Council Tax rebate to help with the rising cost of bills

Debt management support

The citizens advice bureau provides free support to get on top of debt as well as understanding the beneifts you're entitled to

Consumer advice

Martin Lewis' consumer advice website provides trustworthy advice about everything from shopping vouchers to mortgage deals

Who can you contact for help?

If you can't afford household bills or repayments on debits contact the company and explain your situation.
They may agree to reduce your payment for an agreed period of time or set up an payment plan.
Energy companies may offer an affordable payment plan or provide emergency credit to prepayment customers who can't afford to top up.
