Slice of Lime introduce Smart energy meter feature

Our feature analyses your smart meter data and shows you easy ways to use less. It will give you warnings when the consumption exceeds a specific level.

How does our Smart energy meter feature work?

If the consumption in a day exceeds average limits it gives an amber warning. When it exceeds the upper limit it gives a red warning.
If its in the normal range it shows green .
