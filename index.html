What is A Touch Of Lime

A Touch of Lime is an organisation created to help you better manage the cost of living crisis.
From tips and tricks, to our handy chatbox containing a multitude of features including: current deals and offers at a variety of supermarkets, tracking your monthly energy consumption, an analysis of your transactions including potential ways in which you can save money, and a recipe budgeting calculator.

Online banking

Keep up to date with your latest transactions and see exactly where your money is going. We'll provide you with a summary using our open banking software to keep your finances in check

Advice
